# Key Decisions — 2026-06-03 1110

**Session Goal:** Stage the hybrid cash-based medical practice project: research best practices, activate advisors, and build epics/milestones to completion.

## Decisions Made

### Decision 1: Project identity
- **Context:** Needed a name/scope/anchor metric to structure all staging.
- **Options Considered:** CBAHybridCare / FunctionalWellnessHybrid / CBARevenueTransformation; scope options; 4 candidate metrics.
- **Decision:** Name = **FunctionalWellnessHybrid**; scope = **whole-practice transformation** (insurance optimization + cash lines + revenue/cost turnaround combined); anchor metric = **$1M+ gross revenue** (12 mo).
- **Rationale:** Owner selections; matches the strategic pivot.
- **Impact:** Frames all three foundation documents and the release plan.

### Decision 2: Sequencing strategy
- **Context:** Thin capacity + DCR covenant + cash-flow pain require disciplined order.
- **Decision:** Fix RCM first → layer recurring (APCM/RPM/PIR) on existing panel → add high-margin cash lines → scale/optimize. Prefer lease/financed devices; phase CapEx behind monthly DCR checks.
- **Rationale:** Strategy/PMO/Finance advisors — protect the core, collect what's earned before spending, respect the covenant.
- **Impact:** Drives Epics A–I and Milestones M1–M6.

### Decision 3: Advisor activation
- **Context:** Owner asked to activate all relevant agents.
- **Decision:** Distributed 18 advisors (36 files) into 9 department folders; consulted Finance, Growth-n-Revenue, Strategy, PMO, Operations, Marketing, Legal, Human-Psychology to author drafts.
- **Impact:** Local advisor access for all future sessions; frameworks embedded in the documents.

## Learnings & Insights
- Research benchmarks: hybrid ~23% higher revenue; cash-pay 30–40% higher margin & ~65% less admin; DPC membership $75–150/mo; APCM G0556/57/58 = $15.20/$48.84/$107.07/mo; lasers/IV highest margin (IV supplies ~$10/$280 session), body contouring ~40–50%; RCM targets: clean-claim ≥95%, denial <5%, A/R <40, net collection ≥95%.
- Senior cash-pay requires explicit willingness-to-pay validation and Medicare opt-out care — flagged as a key assumption/risk.
